AWAY FROM HOME

At work or at school? It's pretty obvious that you aren't allowed to download extensions onto their computer's Google Chrome/Firefox/Opera/etc installation, that is, if you are fortunate enough to even have any of those browser installed on the computers. However, if your workplace/school hasn’t blocked http://agar.io/, or at least https://agar.io/, you may have an alternative way of using agariomods.

Portable Browsers

If you have a personal computer and any flash drives, you can try downloading Google Chrome Portable, Firefox Portable, or Opera Portable, and installing it onto a flash drive. You can then run the browser on your PC and install Agariomods onto it, so you can use it once you are at work/school. The portable browser will not change anything on the computer it’s plugged into, everything stays in the usb. Note: If you aren’t prohibited from downloading things onto the computers, you can download one of the portable browsers listed above on and install it on your Desktop instead.

Bookmarklet

If your work/school computers have a non-IE browser, you can try our bookmarklet. A bookmarklet is a bookmark that executes a script instead of going to another webpage. First, enable your bookmarks bar, you can google it if you don’t know how, then drag the button below to your bookmarks bar, and it will create a bookmark with a weird name. To use the bookmarklet, you need to go to either http://agar.io/ or https://agar.io/, and BEFORE the page has loaded(everything is still white), you need to click the bookmarklet, or it won’t work correctly. This should start up Agariomods for that session, and everything will be like you had the actual script.
