For thos situations where an installed plugin is not desirable it is possible to use a simple bookmark to apply our modifications to the agar.io game.

Drag this hyperlink, Apply agariomods, to your bookmark bar or read below for instructions.

Add our bookmarklet to your browser. Load up agar.io website. Click on our Apply agariomods bookmark and it will apply agariomods automatically for you.

Firefox
First make sure the Bookmarks Toolbar is visible. Right click on any part of the menu bar and check "Bookmarks Toolbar"
Drag the "Apply agariomods" link onto the Bookmarks Toolbar.

Chrome
First make sure the Bookmarks Toolbar is visible. Click the spanner button at the far right of the menu bar. Then in the tools menu check "Always show bookmark bar"
Drag the "Apply agariomods" link onto the Bookmarks Toolbar.
